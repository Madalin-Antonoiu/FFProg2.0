A guide that has you fully covered.

There are many ways of coding. I focus on a clean, easily readable block-based coding so you can painlessly add or remove features at a fast pace! My guide covers both Front and Back-End Development, aka what's in front of your eyes when you look at a website and what`s running behind the scenes.

Following this guide you will learn how to code and run your own websites from nothing to the final product. No matter if you are a complete beginner or an experienced programmer, this guide has something for you! It`s been designed to be easily soaked in and get fast to the beautiful end product, while learning in the process! Get the basics and form the core, start adding the features you desire and i promise you it will run and look perfect on any device!

Build the core foundation...

Python and its Django library are well known for being actively used by giants like Google, Reddit, YouTube, Facebook, Instagram and others! Simplicity, speed and versatility are at home with Python!

...design the look and hit publish!

HTML&CSS is the bread and butter for website design, while Bootstrap helps tremendously with responsive design and saves alot of time altoghether!

But there`s more...

Throughtout this guide you will also get a solid practice of using Linux's Terminal, Github, PythonAnywhere (for hosting our websites online for free) and couple other techniques.

By this point you probably say to yourself: "I can find all this on Google anyways". And the answer to that is "Yes, your are right". What this guide does is it saves you alot of time otherwise spent searching, figuring out how to incorporate new features or debugging errors, so that you can spend more time coding and less time treating headaches!
